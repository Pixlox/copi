# Copi
Your clipboard copilot. *Understands* your clipboard. All locally.

<img width="500" height="500" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/5c0e9a40-a532-4e99-93fd-5c92cc57fcfb" />

## Why Copi
Your clipboard manager will just show you history, and let you search it. 

Copi's different, it'll let you search by meaning.

## In development
Copi's currently in pre-alpha. It's quite frankly really buggy, slow, and needs a lot of work. Copi works best on macOS for now. If you'd like to contribute, feel free to open a PR!
